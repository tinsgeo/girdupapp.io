How It Works

Simple. Disciplined.
Uncompromising.

GirdUp locks the apps you choose — social media, news, browsers, streaming — until your Bible reading and prayer are done. Every morning. No shortcuts.

01

Choose Your Apps to Lock

During setup, select which apps get locked each morning — Instagram, TikTok, Safari, YouTube, whatever pulls you away before you've been with God. You decide. GirdUp enforces it.

02

Wake Up. Apps Lock.

Every morning GirdUp activates. The apps you chose are inaccessible — not broken, purposed. A daily reminder that you made a commitment and you're being held to it.

03

Read 15 Minutes of Scripture

Open the built-in Bible reader. A timer tracks your reading time. Multiple translations — KJV, NKJV, NIV, ESV, NLT, CSB. Reading plans. Verse highlighting. 15 minutes in full — your mind is being armed.

"Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path."Psalm 119:105
04

Pray

After reading, GirdUp prompts you to pray. Guided prayer, prayer journal, or simply confirm you've prayed. However you approach God — this step is not optional.

05

Apps Unlock. Go Face the Day.

Your chosen apps become available. The world gets the version of you that has already been in the Word. Discerning. Grounded. Girded up.

"Gird up the loins of your mind, be sober, and rest your hope fully upon the grace of Christ."1 Peter 1:13
